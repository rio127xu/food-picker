# 🍔 Fanny Food Battle

A playful food decision game for Fanny.

## 🎮 What is this?

Fanny Food Battle is a lightweight interactive web app that helps decide:

👉 What to eat today

Instead of thinking too much, just battle it out.

---

## ⚡ How it works (V3)

### Step 1: Choose what you want to eat
Select multiple food categories:
- Burger
- Pizza
- Fast Food
- Chinese Food
- Snacks
- Drinks

---

### Step 2: Exclude what you don’t want
Filter out categories you dislike today.

---

### Step 3: Battle Mode

The app will:

- Randomly generate food candidates
- Show 2 options at a time
- You choose the winner
- The loser is removed
- Winner continues to next round

This continues until only one winner remains.

---

## 🏆 Result

The final food becomes:

- Today’s recommendation
- Saved into Favorites

---
